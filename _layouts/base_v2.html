About

Currently, I study Systems Engineering and Computer Science at Washington University in St. Louis. In the last three years, I discovered passions that include entreprenuership, technology, and fitness.

Outside of class, I row six days per week with the Washington University men's varsity crew team. We compete in all major regattas in the midwestern and eastern regions of the United States. In addition, as the president of the team, I ensure the safe transportation of our equipment and team members, lead the executive board, and manage our relations with the St. Louis community.

I am also the director of wute.vc, an organization that hosts speakers in venture capital and entrepreneurship for the Washington University community. We are the primary application processor for a startup accelerator for wustl students called WuStart@OpenDNS.

In my spare time, I enjoy building web and mobile applications, tasting new wines, and camping.

Most recently, I interned with the Accenture Technology Labs in San Jose, CA.

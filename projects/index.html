Projects

Sweep

Sweep is a iOS and web based rowing workout tracker. Here's what it does:

  • Take a photo of an erg screen and auto generate a workout log from the image.
  • Manually record a workout by logging each segment / interval.
  • Save all of your workouts and have them stored with a cloud database.
  • Get analytics about a workout, ie. how your 2k time progressed.
  • Affiliate yourself with a team and then show rankings for a workout on a given date.

Danny Corin and I have been building this app in our spare time. We both row on the Washington University crew team and are rising seniors in engineering.

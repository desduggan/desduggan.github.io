About

about me

Currently, I study Systems Engineering and Computer Science at Washington University in St. Louis. In the last three years, I discovered passions that include entreprenuership, technology, and fitness.

Outside of class, I row six days per week with the Washington University men's varsity crew team. We compete in all major regattas in the midwestern and eastern regions of the United States. In addition, as the president of the team, I actively manage team operations and strategy.

My projects include Parq and Rowing Analytics.

This summer I will be interning in the Bay Area with the Accenture Technology Labs, and when I return in the fall, I plan on working part time with Cultivation Capital as part of the due diligence team.
